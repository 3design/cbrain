Seamless Access To Cloud Computing

CBRAIN provides a place where users can connect computing and cloud resources together to give users a vast pool of computation to acheive their science. CBRAIN has a partnership with The Digital Research Alliance of Canada to provide millions of computing hours for the research com-munity through our Portal.

Make Complex Pipelines Available Via A Standardized GUI

CBRAIN works with an ecosystem of software, making it easy for users to define, test and import their own pipelines into the system. We utilize the easy to use Boutiques JSON Standard to define execution pipelines for automatic import to CBRAIN. You can easily create a standardized GUI for your tool as well

Analyze And View Large Datasets Online

CBRAIN provides a web-interface between users and large datasets. It improves productivity by facilitating online viewing of the pre and post-processed data, thus enabling team members to perform quality assurance remotely without reliance on local software installation. Brainbrowser supports visualization features in CBRAIN.

Simplify Data Transfer And Management

CBRAIN offers a number of Data Providers for storing, organizing, and moving your data seamlessly throughout the CBRAIN ecosystem ensuring large datasets are moved efficiently and securely to perform large-scale computations.

Whether your data starts on your desktop, resides at a computing center or lives out in the cloud, it can easily become part of the CBRAIN data ecosystem.

Automated Workflows And Provenance Tracking

CBRAIN has a multitude of computational pipelines that allow you to perform complex "big data" analytics. The pipelines range from simple conversion operations to neuroimaging to genomics to supply chain simulation modeling.

Users can decide what data to use, what options are needed, and then launch pipelines on large-scale computing rapid processing. In all cases, detailed log files are automatically generated by CBRAIN, making provenance tracking and quality control simpler.

Simplify Training And Teamwork

CBRAIN allows creating team projects and customizing access to different data and projects, By reducing the burden of local software installation, provenance tracking, and a customizable GUI, it improves user experience and reduces the reproducibility risks associated with human error. Team members can remotely verify the postprocessing stages and results, thus making training more efficient.
